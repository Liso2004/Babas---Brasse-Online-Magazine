Password Reset wireframe

Password reset request, confirmation, token, validation, and recovery states.

①

Password reset is a support auth route, linked from login and returning safely to login.

②

Request step collects email and uses neutral confirmation copy to avoid account enumeration.

③

Confirm step includes token validation, password strength, new password, and confirmation fields.

④

States cover email sent, expired token, password mismatch, and reset success redirect.

⑤

Return and support affordances keep editors from getting trapped in the auth flow.

Pin selection is interactive for review.
